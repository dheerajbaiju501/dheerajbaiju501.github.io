Dheeraj Baiju

I am a Pre-Doctoral Fellow at the Visual Computing Lab, IISc Bangalore, where I work with Prof. Anirban Chakraborty on multimodal learning, generative models, and reliable AI evaluation.

Previously, I collaborated with Dr. Prashant Kumar on topological deep learning, Dr. Soumya Sanyal on calibrating language models for long-form question answering, and Dr. Satyam Srivastava at CSIR-CEERI on medical image classification and segmentation.

I completed my bachelor’s in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at BITS Pilani, advised by Prof. Tejasvi Alladi.

Beyond research. I play football and squash, enjoy long-distance running, and recently returned to the piano after a long break.
