Dheeraj Baiju

I am a Research Assistant at the Visual Computing Lab, IISc Bangalore, working with Prof. Anirban Chakraborty on developing DynEval, a holistic evaluation framework for text-to-image models that jointly measures prompt alignment and image quality while providing fine-grained feedback on where generated images fail.

I am also working with Prof. Narendra Ahuja on harnessing multimodal language models for visual representation learning.

I completed my B.E. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at BITS Pilani, where I worked with Prof. Tejasvi Alladi.

Beyond research. I play football and squash, enjoy long-distance running, and recently returned to the piano after a long break.
